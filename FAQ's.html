Frequency Asked Questions...

Do I need an appointment?

Yes you must schedule an appointment before hand.

Can I have an x-ray if i'm pregnant?

No this is not recommended only in emergency situation should you speak to your doctor to see if this is permitted.

What is your hours of operation?

Monday - Friday : Hours 9am - 5pm

Do I need an order from my doctor?

Yes a doctor must order you to have a scan.

Can I eat and drink normally before having contrast injected?

No you must not eat anything from 4 hours before, till after the contrast has been injected.
